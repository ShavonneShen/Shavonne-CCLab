Concept

Inspired by elements in the tarot cards, I coded two graphics: eye and moon.

Eyes represent prophecy, while moon represents mysteries(but unfortunately, I did not code the waxing crescent successfully).

To add to the mysterious atmosphere, the background color changes everytime you click the botton, but will mainly in dark colors.

Design

Eye: Ellipses and circles

Moon: Two ellipses with different inlines and outlines give the basic shape of the full moon

Then I coded the rectangle to shade in order to create the shape of the waxing crescent.

*When making the single moon pattern, the function worked well but when I looped it, the rectangle covered the whole background.

I was confused and finally deleted the rectangle model. Therefore, my moon shapes are actually two sysmetry waxing crescents.
